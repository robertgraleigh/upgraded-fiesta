What I Value

The basic values I use to shape my principles, and guide my daily actions in business and in life

Agility

I believe that agility is central to the success and longevity of any modern technology professional. Being agile, and cultivating an ability to face the uncertain, quickly make decisions, and rapidly de-escalate situations, is essential in today's internet-based world.

Community

I believe in helping others through direct action in my own community. I do this in many ways, but I'm happiest when the answer to "What can I contribute today?" is action-driven, and I'm creating lasting value, rather than just "giving".

Creativity

I believe that creativity is innate in all people, but our ability to leverage creative thinking is often prevented by fear. It's not always the level of creativity, or novelty of a new idea or solution that matters most. It's having the courage to implement it.

Empathy

I believe that empathy is a buzz word for many of us. Starting out with empathy—for customers—for coworkers—for strangers can be difficult. Yet it's essential, and can be so powerful when it's genuine. A human connection can change everything.

Problem-Solving

I believe that effective problem-solving comes from an iterative approach. My intellectual curiosity and drive to identify and solve problems has led me to lean into many unique business obstacles and challenges throughout my career.

Wisdom

I believe we owe it to ourselves and those around us to "pay our successes forward". I actively seek out diverse leaders, from different geneartions, sharing accrued wisdom and experience freely with others, as part of my daily professional approach.
